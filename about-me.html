About me

My journey with technology started early. My first steps into programming were on an Atari 800 XL, where I spent hours writing BASIC programs and saving them to cassette tapes. What began as childhood curiosity quickly evolved into a lifelong career building software.

I formalised my passion at AGH University of Science and Technology in Krakow, studying Applied Computer Science. During my studies, I began my professional career as a Full Stack Developer, working part-time with PHP, JavaScript, and HTML. I later joined Comarch as a Software Engineer, primarily focusing on Java.

While at Comarch, I discovered a deep interest in mobile ecosystems. I taught myself Objective-C and transitioned into the world of iOS development, eventually taking on responsibilities as an iOS Team Lead.

I later joined Codete as a Software Engineer, where I focused on high-scale iOS applications. My most significant project was a geolocation-based social app used by millions of users. Working on this scale required a heavy focus on:

  • Performance: Achieving a consistently low crash rate and high optimization.
  • User Experience: Implementing extensive A/B testing to drive engagement.
  • Scale: Managing complex geolocation data for a global user base.

Following Codete’s acquisition by Exadel, I moved into my current role focusing on internal AI products. Today, I leverage my background in full-stack and mobile engineering to build the next generation of intelligent software tools.
